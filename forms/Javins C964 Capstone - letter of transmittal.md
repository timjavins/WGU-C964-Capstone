Dear Mr. Carter,

Our team has long been committed to delivering cutting‐edge financial analysis tools. In today’s rapidly evolving markets, the ability to harness historical stock data and predict future trends is essential. To address this need, we have developed an integrated platform that automates data collection, preprocessing, feature engineering, model training, and actionable trading strategy implementation.

Using daily data sourced via Yahoo Finance, our solution preprocesses and enriches stock data to compute key features such as volatility, momentum, and return. With the help of an XGBoost regression model, the platform predicts next-day returns and quantifies prediction accuracy using Mean Squared Error. The model’s output forms the core of a trading strategy that generates buy and sell signals, which are then thoroughly backtested against historical performance data.

This comprehensive approach not only streamlines our data pipeline but also equips Global Investment Solutions with the analytical tools required to make data-driven investment decisions and optimize trading strategies. The project is designed for scalability and cost efficiency, with an upfront investment of approximately $14,000 and modest annual maintenance costs.

Thank you for taking the time to review our proposal. We are excited about the opportunity to support Global Investment Solutions in enhancing its market analysis capabilities. Please feel free to reach out if you have any questions or require further details.
